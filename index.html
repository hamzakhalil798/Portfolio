Spothole

Developed "Spothole," an advanced pothole detection system utilizing computer vision and geospatial analysis to enhance road maintenance efficiency. This project leverages a YOLOv8 trained model for accurate pothole detection from road videos.

    • Designed and developed a user-friendly interface using Tkinter for easy uploading and processing of road videos.
    • Trained and integrated YOLOv8 for pothole detection.
    • Implemented real-time pothole detection in the video feed once uploaded.
    • Captured and logged the longitude and latitude of each detected pothole into a CSV file for documentation and further processing.
    • Utilized Folium maps and OSMnx to segment roads into 500-meter intervals for detailed spatial analysis.
    • Analyzed the density of potholes within each 500-meter road segment based on the recorded locations.
    • Generated a final map with a heatmap overlay to visually represent the concentration of potholes across different road segments, aiding in targeted road maintenance.
